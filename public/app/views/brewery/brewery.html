My reason for starting a brewery is because I love the fact I can create and brew any beer my mind can come up with. I started home brewing 5 yrs ago with extract kits moved to all grain and decided I can do a nano brewery 1 bbl system.


I learned how to brew beer by reading books, anything I could find. Started on a beercation brewery trek through Iowa, Minnesota, Nebraska and Wisconsin. Talking to and asking questions to their brewers. Brewers are all willing to share and help other brewers. That's what I really appreciate. Brewers are friendly and open community.


Fall 2016 traveled to Germany and Austria for 2 weeks of brewery and Oktoberfest tours. Comparing their ways of making beers. Many of these breweries hundreds of years old still bottle condition instead of CO2 carbonation.


Finally, the best part of brewing beer is to share it with people who love it and appreciate the quality and taste of my beers.


So try my beers I hope to see you at the locations that will serve my beers. Cheers or as they say in Germany Prost!
