Honey I'm Home

Unique IPA "juicy" citrus aromatics and flavored by 4 different hop varieties and locally harvested raw honey. This is one that will convert many to IPAs that say they don't like them.

Mudslinger

Ebony in color with a rich creamy head. The mellow flavors of various chocolate malts, and real cacao nibs in secondary process are balanced perfectly with the right amount of hops for a bittersweet finish. Chocolate Stout is brewed with award winning Carlisle aquafier natural water.

Dizzy Blonde

Crisp refreshing easy drinking blonde ale with hints of Madagascar bourbon vanilla beans.
